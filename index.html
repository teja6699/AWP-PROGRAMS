ABOUT US

Data Science and Machine learning research center of Centurion University of Technology and Management was established on 15th August 2020. The objectives of this research center are To develop impactful research which can lead to improve businesses, the health and wellbeing of individuals and global communities To offer training and consultancy services to industry and academia To provide access to advanced computing facilities and expertise in Education and Training. To increase academic and research collaborations within and outside the University to achieve the new heights in innovations.

FOCUS AREAS

  • Plant Disease Detection
  • Natural Language Processing
  • Geomatics and Applications
  • Big Data Analytics using Hadoop and MongoDB
  • Extract, transform and Load(ETL)
